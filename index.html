How to Play

The middle starts with $1000. You both start with nothing.
Each round, pick an action secretly.
After 9 rounds, the richer player wins.
First to 3 wins takes the series.

Actions

Add — Double the middle, +$1k if empty.
Steal — Take the middle. Both steal = nothing.
Raid — Take opponent's wealth. Both raid = swap. Blocked by Stash.
Stash — Block raids.
Share — Dump your wealth to middle. Dodges Raid.

Order

Add → Steal → Share → Raid

The machine learned by playing 2,000,000 games against itself.
